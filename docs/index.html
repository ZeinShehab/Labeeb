Lebanon's first sign language translator application

إن اللبيب من الاشارة يفهم

Labeeb aims to address communication challenges faced by the deaf and hard-of-hearing community through the development of a Lebanese Sign Language Translator app. The app offers real-time translation services for both the Arabic alphabet and common Lebanese words.

How to use Labeeb

Symbols

Press on the symbols button and sign your symbols

Gestures

Press on the gesture symbol and sign your gesture until the gesture stops capturing

Combinations

Combination of words are automatically mapped

Copy text

Press on the sentence twice to copy text to clipboard

Clear Text

Clears the translated text

Enlarge text

Check your text in bigger font
